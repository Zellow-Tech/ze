ZelTech

Zellow is a software development company founded in 2022 by two school friends, Shimron and Shahir. With a passion for technology and innovation they established the company with the aim of creating cutting-edge software solutions in a wide range of fields. From the outset, Zellow has been committed to delivering high-quality products and services that meet the needs of their clients. With a team of talented and dedicated developers, Zellow has worked and is working on a variety of projects in areas such as law, education, finance, and media. Today, Zellow continues to grow and innovate, with a focus on delivering exceptional software solutions and services to clients across the globe. Zellow's team remain deeply committed to the company's mission and vision, and are dedicated to maintaining and building world class software for mankind.
